What is the impact of humans on the environment?

Impact on resources

The development of human knowledge is limitless but natural resources are limited, the impact of humans on natural resources makes it gradually exhausted and cannot be regenerated, although humans have gradually replace the use of non-renewable resources such as coal, oil, etc. But for the constant population growth, it does not seem to be enough. Therefore, people need to be more active in exploiting and using, not exploiting arbitrarily and indiscriminately.

People influence their own lives

When a series of factories, vehicles, and life amenities were born to meet people's living needs, but going along with that development is a direct loss to the living environment and no one else is suffering. It is humans who must increasingly bear the consequences. As environmental pollution is increasing, climate change occurs, the greenhouse effect increases, the number of acid rain is increasing, the earth is warming globally, many natural disasters occur, not only causing acid. Ocean degradation, extinction of species, collapse of ecosystems, or loss of biodiversity, when all these consequences occur, means that human life also gradually disappears when there is no more supply. , place to live. Therefore, facing such serious consequences, people need to quickly change their attitudes about environmental protection.

Impact on ecosystems & biodiversity

Garbage, toxic gas, pollution are all that humans bring to mother nature after exploitation. Environmental pollution directly affects ecological balance and destroys biodiversity. Humans exploit and hunt animals and plants in a bluff, to satisfy their momentary desires, which has caused many species of animals and plants to become extinct and in danger of extinction. strains. Moreover, when garbage and dirty water are not treated, discharged directly into the environment, many marine animals eat and die, such as the mass death of corals, or sea turtles, etc. All of these have extremely negative impacts on ecosystems and biodiversity.

Summarize

The environment is so important, directly facing the impacts of humans on the environment. Therefore, protecting the environment is protecting our own lives and future generations on this planet, so even in our daily lives, we need to raise the awareness of ourselves and those around us by the most practical actions.
